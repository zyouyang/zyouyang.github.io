Biography

Hi 💚! I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Dartmouth College, advised by Prof. Soroush Vosoughi. My research interests broadly lie in the application of recommender systems (e.g., graph-based collaborative filtering, click-through-rate prediction) and graph representation learning over various domains (e.g., healthcare, mobile application promotion). In summer 2024, I was fortunate to intern at Microsoft Bing Places as a data scientist intern.
