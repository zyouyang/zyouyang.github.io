Biography

Hi! I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Notre Dame, advised by Prof. Yanfang Ye. My research interests broadly lie in the application of retrieval-augmented large language models, recommender systems (e.g., graph-based), graph representation learning over various domains (e.g., healthcare, mobile application promotion).
